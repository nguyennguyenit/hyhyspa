About Us

HyHy Spa & Massage

HyHy Spa & Massage is a soothing retreat designed to relieve daily fatigue and stress, bringing deep relaxation to both the body and mind while restoring their natural balance.

In a calm and peaceful atmosphere, our experienced therapists provide attentive treatments, carefully tailored to each guest's physical condition and skin needs.

In addition to oil massage and hot stone therapy, our facial care treatments gently address individual skin concerns, helping you relax deeply while enhancing your skin's natural beauty.

Step away from your busy daily life and enjoy a moment of self-care and tranquility at HyHy Spa & Massage.

We sincerely look forward to welcoming you.
